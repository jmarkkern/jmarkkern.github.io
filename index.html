Extracurriculars

Thai Culture Association at UCI

TCA is an organization at UC Irvine seeking to bring together people who appreciate Thai culture. We meet weekly to teach or learn a little more about Thai culture while making friends within the club. The group is very tight-knit which inspired me to stay involved. I helped manage the organization's social media for two years and continue to be an active member since.

Camp Wat Pa

Camp Wat Pa is the only Thai youth leadership program in the US, hosted annually on Labor Day weekend in Los Angeles. I came to this camp 5 years ago as a camper and loved my experience here as I connected with other people from the Thai community. This sense of belonging brings me back as a counselor every year as I want to continue empowering the kids to feel pride in their Thai identity.
