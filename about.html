How could I join art and technology?

Growing up with technology being in every facet of life, I guess it was inevitable for me to end up wanting to work in tech. When I was a kid, I had a passion for art. I was always doodling in the corner of my parents' small restaurant (when I wasn't busy helping them around). My mom would come out of the kitchen and remark on how maybe I would grow up to be a designer.

Fast forward some years, when I was in a STEM-focused school program, I thought I just had to study some kind of science, so when I went to university, I ended up majoring in Informatics! From there, I discovered user interface design, and my little art muscles were working again, and I was excited to do my homework for once. Since then, I learned about designing and engineering whole systems or products, thus begins my journey into UX/UI!
